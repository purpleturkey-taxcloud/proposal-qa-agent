Proposal QA

This tool is designed to help you catch PandaDoc issues before sending a proposal out to a prospect. Once your PandaDoc is ready to send, just upload the PDF to this grader tool and it will give you a readout of any mistakes, missing fields, or areas of improvement. These should be fixed before sending the contract for signature.
